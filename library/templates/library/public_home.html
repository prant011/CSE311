About Our System

Our Library Management System is designed to streamline the operations of university libraries. It provides a user-friendly interface for both students and librarians.

  • Efficient Book Management: Organize and track books effortlessly.
  • Student Tracking: Manage student records and borrowing history.
  • Real-time Updates: Get instant notifications for overdue books and fines.
  • Secure Access: Role-based authentication for secure access.
  • Comprehensive Reports: Generate detailed reports for library statistics.
